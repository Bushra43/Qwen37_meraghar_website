Our Mission & Vision

15+
Years of Service

Building a Brighter Tomorrow, One Child at a Time

Founded in 2010, Mera Ghar (meaning "My Home") is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to providing shelter, education, and love to orphaned and underprivileged children across India.

We believe that every child deserves a safe home, quality education, nutritious meals, and the warmth of a caring family. Our holistic approach ensures that each child grows into a confident, capable, and compassionate individual.
